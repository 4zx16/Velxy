About Velxy Studios, Inc.

Velxy Studios, Inc. is a creative game studio focused on delivering polished, age-appropriate games for kids and teens. We craft imaginative, T-rated experiences that balance accessibility with challenge, ensuring fun gameplay that’s safe and enriching.

Our titles are built with vibrant visuals, intuitive mechanics, and engaging stories that capture the interests of younger audiences without sacrificing quality. Whether it’s action, adventure, or educational gameplay, we keep things fresh, creative, and player-first.

At Velxy, we’re all about making memorable games that spark curiosity, inspire imagination, and keep players coming back for more.
